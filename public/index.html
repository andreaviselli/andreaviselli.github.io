Insight: Comparing predictive ability in presence of instability over a very short time

Andrea Viselli published on 2025-09-23
The paper Comparing predictive ability in presence of instability over a very short time, co-authored with my former Ph.D. advisors Fabrizio Iacone and Luca Rossini, is now published in The Econometrics Journal. Abstract We consider forecast comparison in the presence of instability when this affects only a short period of time. We demonstrate that global tests do not perform well in this case, as they were not designed to capture very short-lived instabilities, and their power vanishes altogether when the magnitude of the shock is very large.
